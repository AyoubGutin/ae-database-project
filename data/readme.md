The data in this folder is specific to the A&E Attendances and Emergency Admissions 2025-26 collection.

It collects the total number of admissions for all A&E types, number discharged, admitted, or transferred within four hours of arrival. Waits over four hours for admissions following decisions to admit are also in the data.

Data is broken down by System and at provider organisation level.

Citation: https://www.england.nhs.uk/statistics/statistical-work-areas/ae-waiting-times-and-activity/ae-attendances-and-emergency-admissions-2025-26/
